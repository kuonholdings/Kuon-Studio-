BMC Guitar

The BMC Guitar is a study in resonance, stability, and controlled acoustic performance. The project rethinks the acoustic guitar to achieve greater playability through construction logic, material discipline, and reduced physical resistance.

Focusing on precision rather than ornament, this work explores how sound is generated at instrument scale. It demonstrates KUON Studio's commitment to refining tools for performance through reduction and material clarity.
