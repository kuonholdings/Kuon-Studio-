BMC Guitar

Our instrument research is grounded in the belief that a guitar should offer stability, control and precision while maintaining the warmth of acoustic resonance. This project rethinks the geometry, tension and material logic of the acoustic guitar to improve playability and sonic clarity.

  • Explores lower string tension and controlled resonance for improved performance.
  • Focuses on material logic and construction that encourage clarity over loudness.
  • Emphasises tactile feedback and reduced formal noise for a more intimate relationship between player and instrument.
