Sleep Speaker

The Sleep Speaker investigates sound in relation to the body, rest, and the human nervous system. Designed for proximity and low‑light domestic use, it explores how calming auditory environments can support rest and wellbeing.

This study extends KUON Studio's interest in how sound affects the human condition. It considers the role of sound in sleep and recovery and tests how physical form can encourage a gentle listening relationship.
