Kuon Studio is a developing practice concerned with sound, form, and the structures that give them lasting presence.

The studio sits between object, instrument, and space, with work developing across audio pieces, musical tools, and architectural ideas for listening.

This temporary site marks the beginning of that broader body of work. A fuller presentation will follow in due course.
