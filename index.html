Sound, given structure.

Kuon Studio is a sound architecture practice concerned with how sound becomes real in the world.

Its work spans sound at origin, sound in architecture, and sound as complete form.

Works

The work is structured across three conditions: origin, architecture, and completion.

Guy Traviss

Kuon Studio is the practice of Guy Traviss.

Trained in music and recording, his work developed through instrument design, acoustic research, and manufacturing across Europe and China.

This experience spans the full path of sound — from its formation in instruments, through its capture in recording, to its realisation within architectural space.

The studio brings these domains together into a single line of work concerned with how sound is given form.
