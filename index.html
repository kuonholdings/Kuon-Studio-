Overview

KUON Studio explores how sound is generated, shaped, reproduced, and lived with.

The practice works across instruments, signal tools, listening objects, and spatial systems, with selected studies extending into wellbeing and the human condition.

Current work includes Neostein, Maso, instrument development, and research-led audio concepts.
