Sleep Speaker

Designed around rest, proximity and low‑light use, the Sleep Speaker is a concept that explores how sound can support the nervous system. This object delivers focused, enveloping audio while remaining visually unobtrusive in the bedroom environment.

  • Combines acoustic delivery with a gentle lighting element to support sleep rituals.
  • Proximity sensor and low‑light display minimise distraction while maximising comfort.
  • Investigates sound’s role in rest and recovery through ongoing research and prototyping.
