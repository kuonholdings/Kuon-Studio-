Profile

Guy Traviss is a designer working across sound, objects, and spatial systems. He founded KUON Studio to explore how sound is generated, shaped, reproduced, and lived with.

His practice spans product development, acoustic environments, and research‑led concepts, with current work including instruments, signal tools, listening objects, integrated systems, and wellbeing‑related projects. Based between Taipei and London, Guy brings a cross‑cultural perspective to design, balancing technical precision with human experience.

K UON Studio serves as the public platform for this body of work, connecting individual projects into a coherent practice and providing a basis for future research and collaboration.
