Profile

Guy Traviss is a designer working across sound, objects and spatial systems. Through KUON Studio he develops products, concepts and research‑led studies that connect acoustic generation, signal transformation, listening environments and human experience.

Operating between Taipei and London, his practice combines industrial design, acoustic thinking and a deep interest in the human nervous system. Current work includes instrument development, domestic audio objects, integrated sound systems and exploratory projects related to rest, focus and acoustic well‑being.
