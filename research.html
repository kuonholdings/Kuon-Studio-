Research

Research at KUON Studio treats design as both practice and enquiry. Our current areas of interest include acoustic sound generation, signal transformation, domestic listening environments, sound as an environmental condition, and the role of sound in rest, focus, and wellbeing.

This section will evolve to include short writings, themes, and notes as our research develops. Select an item from the menu to explore our research themes and notes.
