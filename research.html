Research Overview

We treat design as both practice and enquiry. Alongside products and applied work, the studio develops longer‑term questions around sound, material, perception and the human condition. Current areas of interest include acoustic sound generation, signal transformation, listening in domestic space, sound as an environmental condition and the role of sound in rest, focus and wellbeing.

This space will grow to include short texts, project notes, talks and research‑led studies. These pieces will articulate the theoretical underpinnings of our practical work and outline future directions.
