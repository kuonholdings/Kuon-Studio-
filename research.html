Approach

KUON Studio treats design as both practice and enquiry.

Alongside products and applied work, the studio develops longer-term questions around sound, material, perception, and the human condition.

Current areas of interest include acoustic sound generation, signal transformation, listening in domestic space, sound as an environmental condition, and the role of sound in rest, focus, and wellbeing.

Over time, this section can include short texts, project notes, talks, and research-led studies.

Current Themes

Acoustic Generation

How sound begins physically, through instruments, materials, and direct interaction.

Signal Transformation

How sound changes across analogue and digital systems, interfaces, and tools.

Listening in Space

How objects, rooms, and systems affect the experience of hearing.

Wellbeing & Condition

How sound relates to rest, focus, protection, and everyday life.
