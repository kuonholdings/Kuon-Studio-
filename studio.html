Studio Overview

Kuon Studio is a design practice working across sound, objects and spatial systems. We follow sound through its full journey: from human performance and acoustic generation, through signal and transformation, into objects, environments and the conditions of everyday life.

Our work spans instruments, analogue and digital tools, listening objects, integrated systems and research‑led concepts. Some projects become commercial products. Others operate as prototypes or proofs of a wider design position.
