Studio

KUON Studio is a design practice working across sound, objects, and spatial systems. Our work follows sound through multiple states: from performance and physical generation, through signal and transformation, into objects, environments, and everyday life.

The studio operates as both a platform for product development and a field of enquiry where instruments, signal tools, listening objects, integrated systems, and research-led concepts can co‑exist.
