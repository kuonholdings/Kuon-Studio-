Signal Pedal

This compact signal tool distills the guitar pedal to its essentials. Rather than adding complexity, the design focuses on tactile control and deliberate transformation of sound. The pedal makes the signal path legible, allowing musicians to sculpt tone with precision and confidence.

  • Reduces the interface to a small number of purposeful controls.
  • Emphasises tactile feedback and material clarity.
  • Designed for manufacturability while retaining a refined object language.
