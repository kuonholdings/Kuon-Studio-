Signal Pedal

The Signal Pedal explores sound transformation at the level of electrical signal. Reducing the signal path to a small number of deliberate controls, it offers a tactile and legible interface for shaping sound.

This project demonstrates KUON Studio's approach to precision, clarity, and musical utility in product design. It studies how transformation can be expressed through form and interface without adding excess complexity.
