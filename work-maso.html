Maso

Maso is a domestic listening object that embodies KUON Studio's philosophy of restraint, material clarity, and sonic atmosphere. It explores how sound can be reproduced and lived with in the home, translating technical audio thinking into a quiet, tactile object language.

This project demonstrates our approach to designing sound objects that balance fidelity with presence. Maso is conceived not only as a product but as a study in domestic listening and the role of sound in daily life.
