Maso

Maso is a collection of domestic sound objects that translate technical audio thinking into calm, tactile, liveable forms. Each object reduces visual noise and invites active listening, prioritising material clarity and resonance over decorative complexity.

  • Sound objects designed for domestic intimacy and acoustic presence.
  • Explores how sound is reproduced and lived with, rather than simply “played back.”
  • Products are intentionally minimal to focus attention on resonance, proportion and material behaviour.
