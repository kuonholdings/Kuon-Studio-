Maso

A domestic object expression of the studio’s thinking on sound, restraint, atmosphere, and material clarity.

Early Product Direction / Available by Enquiry

Overview

Maso is a product-facing expression within KUON Studio. It translates the studio’s broader work on sound, restraint, and human experience into objects intended for domestic life.

Brief

Create objects that feel calm, considered, and liveable, while carrying a deeper logic rooted in listening, material discipline, and the physical presence of sound in space.

Design Intent

Maso is not presented as an isolated ecommerce brand at this stage. Its credibility comes partly from the objects themselves, and partly from the wider ecosystem of projects that explain why they are so considered.

Within KUON Studio

Maso sits within the studio’s work on objects and space. The surrounding projects — from instruments to signal tools to research-led studies — provide context for its object language and help frame it as an authored product line rather than an isolated launch.

What this project demonstrates

  • Domestic object thinking
  • Atmosphere and restraint
  • A path from studio practice to early commercial product
