Work Overview

Our work spans instruments, signal tools, listening objects and integrated systems. Each project demonstrates a different aspect of how sound can be made physical, legible and intentional.

  • Maso: Domestic sound objects exploring tactility, calm technology and acoustic presence.
  • Guitar: An acoustic study emphasising resonance, control and material logic.
  • Pedal: A compact signal tool focusing on tactile control and sonic transformation.
  • Sleep Speaker: A research‑led concept designed around rest, proximity and low‑light use.
  • Neostein: Integrated systems architecture for listening environments.
