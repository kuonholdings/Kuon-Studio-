Overview

KUON Studio works across the life of sound — from its generation and shaping to its reproduction in objects, spaces, and daily life.

Some projects are commercial in intent. Others are applied studies, prototypes, or demonstrations of a wider design position. Together they give context to one another.

Performance

BMC Guitar

A study in resonance, stability, and controlled acoustic performance.

Signal

Signal Pedal

A compact signal tool exploring tactile control and sonic transformation.

Objects and Space

Maso

A domestic object expression of the studio’s thinking on sound, restraint, and atmosphere.

Neostein

Integrated listening systems and architectural deployment at a larger scale.

Human Experience

Sleep Speaker

A research-led study in proximity, rest, low-light interaction, and acoustic wellbeing.
