Neostein

Integrated listening systems and architectural deployment at a larger scale.

Applied Commercial Work

Overview

Neostein represents the studio’s interest in sound at system level: how audio is reproduced, deployed, and lived with across domestic and architectural environments.

Brief

Develop a coherent product and system language for integrated sound, bridging performance expectations, practical deployment, and a more thoughtful relationship between technology and space.

Design Intent

The project treats sound reproduction not as isolated hardware, but as part of a wider environmental experience. It shows how the studio can work from object scale to larger integrated systems.

Within KUON Studio

Neostein provides the applied system-level context for the practice, complementing the more intimate object language of Maso and the research-led work on human condition.

What this project demonstrates

  • System-level sound thinking
  • Architectural and integration context
  • A commercial application of the studio’s wider philosophy
