Neostein

Neostein is our systems architecture for listening environments. It brings together hardware, software and spatial design to create integrated sound systems that are calm, durable and beautifully unobtrusive.

  • Develops distributed audio infrastructures for domestic and architectural projects.
  • Balances performance with aesthetic restraint and ease of maintenance.
  • Demonstrates the studio’s ability to think at system scale as well as object scale.
