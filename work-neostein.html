Neostein

Neostein represents KUON Studio's thinking at system scale, integrating audio into architectural space. It explores how sound can be embedded and distributed in built environments, balancing technical infrastructure with aesthetic clarity.

This project demonstrates our capacity for integrated audio systems and spatial listening experiences. It sits between product design and architectural acoustics, showing how sound can occupy the spaces we inhabit.
